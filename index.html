        SpringFest is the annual social and cultural festival of IIT Kharagpur, the pioneer of the elite Engineering institutions in India. Currently in its 64st edition, Spring Fest has been celebrating the true spirit of youth for over six decades. Springfest is one of the largest Social and Cultural Fests in India. Embodying the true spirit of youth, Spring Fest provides a platform for young talent from all over India to showcase their varied talents. Spring Fest is a celebration with responsibility. A splendid platform for the spectacular display of the diverse talents of today's youth, we have conducted different activities both online and offline to promote same.


With a plethora of events in the genres of Dance, Music, Movie making , Fashions, sports, Photography,theatre, Dramatics, Literary, quizzing and debating, Fine Arts and Social events, Spring Fest is proud to India's largest social and cultural fest.
Spring Fest is an avenue to embrace the fun and frolic embedded with professional performances from India and abroad along with an addressal to the social responsibility with its theme
